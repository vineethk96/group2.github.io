About Us

There are many travelers and bird enthusiasts who enjoy hiking and exploring in national parks, including some who have limited knowledge about birds. They may need to access a distribution map of a specific bird species to fulfill their exploration interests or require a brief introduction to an unfamiliar bird to learn more. Our website can initially address this need.
